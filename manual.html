GREETINGS👋🏻

Hey there, friend! Welcome to AllerSafe Recipe ⭐️ where cooking is fun, safe, and allergy-friendly. Our cozy community is here to greet you with big smiles and warm hugs 🤗 . Whether you’re avoiding nuts, dairy, gluten, or anything else, we’ve got tasty recipes made just for you. Dive in, explore, and let’s make cooking a joyful adventure together! 🍲❤️

⚙️ MAIN FEATURES ⚙️

  • Filter recipes based on allergens (e.g., nuts, dairy, gluten).
  • View detailed recipe instructions and nutritional information.
  • Save your favorite recipes for quick access later.
  • Add your own recipe for others to view here.

🥗 MANUAL 🥗

  1. Open the app and explore the recipe list on the homepage.
  2. Use the Filter option to exclude recipes containing allergens.
  3. Click a recipe card to view ingredients and step-by-step instructions.
  4. Save recipes you love by clicking the Favorite button.

📌 RULES 📌

  • Be Honest with Your Allergy Information
  • Use the App Responsibly
  • Do Not Share False Information
  • Respect Other Users
  • No Misuse of the Platform
  • Seek Medical Advice When Necessary as this app is a guide, not a replacement for professional medical advice. Consult a doctor if unsure.
  • Follow Community Standards
